Message from Zushi Puzzle

Traditional, fresh sushi fused with art. Try a new sushi experience.

  • Experienced Chef
  • Free Delivery
  • Catering

Picture: Sashimi Combo Plate. Come see the home of the best sushi in the bay area where the sushi is art. Run by Chef Roger Chong as seen on KRON 4 morning news on July 3, 2002. Besides amazing sushi and Japanese dinner combos, we also also Lunch Specials, including Sushi Bar Specials, Donburi and Lunch Plate Specials with teriyaki. Please call for catering information. Free Delivery within a close distance.

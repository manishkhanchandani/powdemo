 "Wow!! So good I didn't want to leave." by wongwahching (4 reviews)
May 10, 2009 - Likes it - In town with my wife for the AIA convention and thought I'd check this little hole in the wall out.
Line up is to be expected and definitely worth it.
The neighborhood is a little sketchy... but that might be a good thing, cause it keeps the tourist away.
We ordered a cup of gumbo, the Shrimp and Grits, and a the Sloppy Joe. What can I say. Best meal of our trip. Seriously, I'm fantasizing about that gumbo right now.
The flavors were so rich yet so balanced that I'm really in awe of this food. I've never before had gumbo or grits before, but even an idiot can tell this is food made from passion and love.
This is a place for foodies who like simple food done incredibly well.

User reviews of Bistro Aix

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 "Perfect for a romantic date..." by Nancy Hom (4 reviews)
March 22, 2009 - Likes it - We found this great place around the corner of another restaurant we left because it was too full.
When we got inside, the host took us to their back patio for seating. It was a really good idea.. despite the cold weather, the cute enclosed patio was warm from the heat lamps and very cozy with the lighting and candles.
My man is a picky french eater and he was extremely happy with this place. We had the tempura calamari for starters.. he had the red wine duck leg confit.. and I had the boullabaise.. it was absolutely divine. Their fresh baked breads were perfect and we were totally waiting for more! For dessert, we had the tarte tatin. Prices were totally reasonable (unless you have a lots of wine like we did)
It was a great meal and very romantic. We definitely plan to come here again!

 "Excellent dinner option in Marina" by Fenn (7 reviews)
December 22, 2008 - Likes it - I was only in SF for 2 weeks, but the meal I had here was one of the best ones of my visit.
I had the Grilled rib-eye with Maitre'd butter, fries and watercress salad - Simple, but executed _extremely_ well.
The steak was perfect, fries fresh and seasoning/sides spot on. It's hard to describe the exact aspects of what made this meal so good, but I guess in summary: They got every bit _right_.
The salad with warmed goats cheese was equally good too.
Highly recommended.

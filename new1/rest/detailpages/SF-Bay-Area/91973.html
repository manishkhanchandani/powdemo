 "Tadich Grill is a true San Francisco institution." by Barry Shulman (21 reviews)
December 11, 2008 - Likes it - It still looks, feels and tastes the same as when I first visited there in the 60s during college, 70s for business, and 80s just for great food. The restaurant reflects the unique fun and historic experience that is San Francisco, drawing heavily on its Gold Rush roots.
It is an eccentric place, that much is true. But this 150-year-old, continuously open restaurant serves some of the best Seafood anywhere. Located in the financial district, Tadich Grill almost always has a line during prime time, takes no reservations, has waiters who some consider abrasive, looks like it is from the past, and is not cheap.
JetSetWay.com Tip - That being said, don’t miss it! There's a good reason it has been around for so long. Its unequalled charm and excellent food keep people coming back decade after decade.
As it was Allyn’s birthday, we each started with a Bloody Mary which was as good as it gets. Their San Francisco sourdough bread is such a treat, that it is served in restaurants all over town. My clam chowder and crab salad was superb, and her smoked trout was terrific.

Recommend this review

 "In a class of its own !!!" by Mike Ahmadi (15 reviews)
November 23, 2008 - Really likes it - I had been wanting to eat at this ridiculously famous San Francisco Landmark for years.  A meeting in the financial district which ended just before 11 AM gave me a chance to get there and be 5th in line at the door.
First of all, the decor and ambience are utterly unique.  You have to see it to get it.  It is neither pretentious nor overly casual.  I love it !
Each table is adorned with a section of Boudin Sourdough Bread, and IT IS TO DIE FOR !!!  I could not stop eating this bread.  It is crusty with a mild sour flavor and chewy, airy texture.
I had the Lobster Thermidor special and I could swear they put more lobster in the shell than is actually found in a lobster.  I could not finish it, and I am a lobster fanatic.  It was expertly prepared and I could not believe how reasonably priced it was.
My mouth is watering just thinking of going back there.  If you ever have a chance to eat there absolutely take it.  It is well worth it !

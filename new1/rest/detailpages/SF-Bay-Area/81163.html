May 27, 2009 - Likes it - Enjoyed this place. Had a light, casual business lunch here this week a liked it quite a bit. Good salads & light fare. I didn't have the sandwhiches here ( or "bocadillos") but they sure looked great and seemed to be very popular. I liked the classic Eames wire-based chairs and cool feeling the place has.

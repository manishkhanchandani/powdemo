Giorgio's Italian Food & Pizzeria - Milpitas Lively chatter and the aroma of garlic are the first things you notice when you walk in the door of Giorgio's Italian Food & Pizzeria. The interior is warm, friendly, and classically 'Italian' (complete with red-checkered tablecloths). It is perfect for romantic couples, as well as the entire family. The restaurant has an open kitchen, where patrons can watch the cooks 'hand toss' the pizza dough and prepare entire meals. Giorgio's has become the pizza 'mecca' of the South Bay area. Don't look for yuppie pizza here. We feature authentic Italian pie that can be topped with your choice of items or one of the house combos.

 "The food!" by Princess of Kisses (1 review)
May 27, 2009 - Likes it - Be sure to make reservations. Nothing worse than coming in here as a walk-in and being told that the wait is 2 hours. Everything is delicious. The service ballet of the waiters and bus-boys is precise and impeccable. The ambiance and decor is nice without pretension.
It can get a little loud. No, I don't mind to be forced to sit within
earshot of yet another windbag on a first-date extolling his uncommon humanity and wealth. But, it can't be helped as the tables are close together and people are there to be seen (and heard, apparently).
So, definitely next time when you go: Never mind the movers and shakers, the gold diggers and players, the high priced call-girls, cougars and posers. For Gods sake! Just go for the food! :)

 "Trully a great restaurant" by linda-payne35 (1 review)
February 20, 2009 - Likes it - I love Greek food and have to say that aside from Milos in New York, this is one of the best Greek restaurants I have ever been to. Everything that I had the night I ate here was phenomenal - including delicious moussaka, tzatsiki, saganaki and baklava - of course. I like really traditional Greek food - none of this "fusion" or "Mediterrenean" stuff (I refuse to believe that's really like yiayia's cooking) - and this place gets it right.
Intimate and semi-rustic setting; would definitely come here again and again and again and again...

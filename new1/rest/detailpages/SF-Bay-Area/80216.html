 "If you love meat you have to try it here" by Ostflicka (4 reviews)
November 17, 2008 - Really likes it - So I have to say I've been here twice this year.  Crazy when I look back and think those two meals cost more then eating out for 6 months of the year.
That being said I love it here.  Perfect place to dress up and share a wonderful meal with friends or a loved one.
The food is to die for.  The steaks especially.  The sides dishes though speak for themselves.  
Macaroni and cheese with truffle oil is so rich, its such a treat.
Wonderful place for birthdays or special occasions.  We dressed in semi formal attire on our visits, but we did see some people not dress up at all.  Felt though as if in this atmosphere almost wrong not to try and complement it .
Be prepared to spent at least $200 per couple without drinks.  If you drink or have a more expensive cut of beef then be prepared to spend about $400-$500 for the evening.
Oh also need to say love the cotton candy.  They serve with every meal before dessert.  There has been a different flavor both times I went.

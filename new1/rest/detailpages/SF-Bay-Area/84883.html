 "So, So" by IAMGAYLORD (2 reviews)
November 03, 2008 - Likes it - I went there with my Partner for a special meal. The table they set us at was just fine until the high picked squeal of the people at the next table. That kind of thing is never the restaurants problem but the food is. My partner ordered Tuna w/ some kind of rice and Mac and Cheese; I ordered ribs and the Lamb and veggie spring rolls. The food here is usually really good, this time it was just so, so. The ribs had a weak tomato based sauce and was kind of fatty and not the hot (temperature). The Tuna had to be sent back because it was cold in the middle. They brought it back and it seemed better. Our waitress was nice enough but she seemed to busy for the amount of tables she had.
It was in the OK but more like So, So.

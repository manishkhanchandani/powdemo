Message from Nation's

A local favorite since 1952 and home of the original Giant Hamburger

  • 24 Locations in the Bay Area
  • World Famous Hamburgers
  • Delicious Desserts

When you go out to eat, you have many restaurant choices, so Nation's gives you many choices in one restaurant. We've never been accused of skimping. After all, we have to live up to the GIANT name, so you get lots of high-quality food and lots of value for your dining dollar. Nation's is not fast food. You order. We cook. And you enjoy our food the way it was meant to be enjoyed: Hot, fresh, and cooked just for you. It may take a few minutes to get your order, But it's well worth the short wait. In addition to our famous burgers, we serve breakfast all day at most locations, as well as delicious homemade pies.

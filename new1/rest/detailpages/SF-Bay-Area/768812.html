Message from Fiddler's Green

Private Parties, Great Music, And Laid Back Pub!

  • Upstairs Room With Dance Floor
  • Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
  • Friendly Staff And Relaxed Bar

Fiddler's Green, located at the Wharf, is an Irish run bar and restaurant. The restaurant caters for all tastes with fresh salads, pitas served with stews, Sunday roasts, and of course, your traditional Irish dishes including Shepard's Pie and Corn Beef and Cabbage. Fiddler's Green also offers live music throughout the entire week with different artists and DJs . We also feature a free private room upstairs that is great for any fun occasion including birthdays, holiday and company parties. So come on in or call us to set up your next private party!

User reviews of Stefano Cafe

 "Perfect lasagna." by j (47 reviews)
May 27, 2009 - Likes it - Been once.
Mixed salad with good parma shavings and grape tomatoes -- fresh and fine.
Lasagna was PERFECT. I mean that. The lasagne were fresh and cooked to the exact right second...not al dente, but firm, and the sauce was very good, robust but still subtle.
Pizza was...well my associate (she's fussy about food, a real gourmet, unlike me) thought it was great. I found the sauce a little sweet, but the basil was fresh. The crust reminds me of New Haven pizza. I'm not sure I'd order it again since there's a lot on the menu to explore, but the pizza is better than average.
I'll go back for sure.

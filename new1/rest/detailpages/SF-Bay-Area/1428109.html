User reviews of Artisan Bistro

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 mobile review by James S. (1 review)
June 24, 2009 - Likes it - As a bit of a foodie, I enjoy living in Lafayette, surrounded by many top restaurants. However, even I was blown away the first time I made it to Artisan Bistro. The outstanding level of cuisine combines with an immaculate level of service to make what is quite possibly the best restaurant for miles. Every dish my party sampled displayed an unprecedented attention to detail and balance, yet all flavor remained unmuddled, with fresh ingredients playing a dominant role. Service was perhapsthe best I have ever recieved, with genuinely pleasant, knowledgeable waitstaff, and very personable managers. I will be returning often, and recommend you try it for yourself. Somewhat pricey, but well worth the cost. My best dining experience for years.

 "Outstanding" by william_leyva (1 review)
March 23, 2009 - Likes it - I've been to Artisan in a couple of occasions and these guys are up to something. The food is in a class by itself for the East Bay. I particularly enjoyed the mussels and the lamb. The service was great for the area, bur it can be improved. I've never had a more delicious Foie Gras Terrine and that includes the French Laundry. I thing we will be hearing from this restaurant for a while. Make sure you have reservations, mainly on week ends including Sunday.

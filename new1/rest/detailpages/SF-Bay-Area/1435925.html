 "Serious Disappointment" by BAM (1 review)
April 11, 2009 - Doesn't like it - Visited Shirasoni last night to 'celebrate' my mothers birthday.(Celebrate in quotes here as the wait staff forgot it was her birthday).
Our party of 4 was more interested in the sushi than the at-the-table cooking so we decided to be seated at the sushi bar. After placing orders for cocktails at the bar we were seated for roughly 15 minutes without receiving a drink, menu, or passing glance from the wait staff. The bartender admitted to 'forgetting' our drink order and couldn't pour a beer or mix a cocktail to save her life (seriously, half a glass of foam? I learned to pour a beer better in high school).
We eventually caught the attention of a waitress who gave us two menus for our four people, no water, no offers of appetizers, and about 10 seconds of total contact with us. We hailed another waitress after another 10 minutes to place an order, and waited what seemed like an eternity to actually have food arrive. Ironically, the fillet mingnon my father ordered arrived a good 5 minutes before our sushi and sashimi orders.
The service was horrendous, the food made nothing better.
If you're a fan of good meat: Go elsewhere. My dad will literally eat anything that moos and polished off his rice and veggies before finishing the meat. Overcooked strips of stir fried fillet mignon that could literally pass as stew meat.
If you're a fan of Sushi go across the street to Kanpai. Anything with 'crab' as an ingredient here is faux crab meat, the sashimi is covered in sauce so you can't taste it, though I'm not exactly sure I'd want to taste the -real- fish there anyway. I ordered a Kara roll that was drenched in soy and smoke sauces, the salmon slices atop it were a sickly color of pink and the 'soft shell crab' appeared to be tempura battered faux crab meat. My sister ordered a spicy roll and the only thing you could taste was the chile sauce they covered it in.
The most disturbing thing by far was sitting at the sushi bar watching plated sushi sit for upwards of 15 minutes before being sent to a table.
Apprehensible service, incredibly poor quality of ingredients. A real disappointment.
Though if you're in to spending 25$+ a plate to watch someone play with your food, you may actually enjoy it.

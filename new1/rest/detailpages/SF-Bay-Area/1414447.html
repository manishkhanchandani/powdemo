 "Smell the sourdough..." by Gina Q. (257 reviews)
January 13, 2009 - Likes it - I wish I had more time to walk around this amazing bread store. All we had time for was to grab a few round loaves of sourdough bread and a few cans of clam chowder before we had to meet back up with the rest of our group. The place was packed with everyone getting a late lunch or early dinner and coffee at the bar. The store was cool with lots of different gadgets and such, but the prices seemed a bit steep. Even the clam chowder was a bit overpriced. I think it was $4 or $5 a can. I would have liked to go on the tour, which was only $3, but didn't have the time. I guess the good thing about that is since I live in LA I can go to the Boudin Bakery tour whenever I want at Disneyland. One good thing I can say about this place is that the sourdough bread samples were awesome. Very sour and fulfilled my San Francisco sourdough craving for the moment. The only other thing that was missing in that moment would have been a great glass of wine and cheese.

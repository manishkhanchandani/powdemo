 "Big Time Little Fish Shack" by heartnsoul10011 (13 reviews)
March 17, 2009 - Likes it - We discovered Woodhouse Fish Company when another couple recommended it to us. Now it's one of our staple restaurants. The menu is full of very reasonably-priced fantastic fresh and fried seafood dishes. The fried clams are always a hit. The fish tacos are filled with super crispy and generous-sized pieces of fish and topped with a chipotle sauce that has a kick. Each night, they have a fresh fish special. Every sandwich on the menu is amazing. Don't forget to order a side of onion rings! Can you tell I've been there tons of times and tried everything? Trust me, this place is great.

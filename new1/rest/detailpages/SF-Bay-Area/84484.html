 "Great service, medium food" by j (47 reviews)
August 22, 2008 - Likes it - I ate lunch there, accidentally left my credit card so had dinner the next day when I went to pick it up.
Lunch was very good, both dim sum and regular fare. The service was outstanding (you don't have *a* server, but the whole staff co-operates on all diners, to great effect) and friendly.
The chow fun was nicely executed and tasty, the string beans with minced pork were cooked perfectly, though the black bean sauce seasoning was unsubtle.
Dinner seemed overpriced, lunch about right, though the dim sum were less expensive than many.
Definitely worthwhile at lunch or any meal if you are in a hurry, so the shining service can show off their chops.

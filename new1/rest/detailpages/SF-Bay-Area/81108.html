 "Awesome pizza outside Berkeley" by GitaGrrl (1 review)
March 04, 2009 - Likes it - Would Blondie's still be great if it was somewhere other than sharing a wall with Rasputin Records? Would Rasputin still be destination if one couldn't score a cheap and tasty pizza lunch? Do we really care? Nope. Blondie's is an an oasis of pizza and a soda in the middle of the tourist-crawling area near Union Square. There are no tables (ok, there's some downstairs, but who wants to sit there?), the service is fast as long as you don't get fancy and it remains cheap - which is a blessing. Love Blondie's, please don't ever change.

Recommend this review

 "The perfect Pizza place to be next to Rasputin's music" by William Wetmore (51 reviews)
January 13, 2009 - Likes it - I discovered Blondies by riding the elevator to the LP floor of Rasputin's music shop... the elevator smelled of fresh pizza, and the attendant told me that they adjoined Blondie's pizza.
With all the food choices in San Francisco, honestly, Pizza wasn't on the top of my list. However, one evening, tired of eating every type of Asian food under the sun, and having been joined in town by my wife who was tired from her flight, we did the quick and simple thing, and picked up a slice at Blondies near our hotel. The Vegetarian pizza on whole wheat crust was marvelous. And, it was very cheap to boot. I'd recommend this pizza to anyone.

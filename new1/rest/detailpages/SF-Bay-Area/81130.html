Message from Blue Plate

Both down-home and sophisticated, this bastion of comfort food offers a Mission twist on California cuisine.

  • Local Organic Ingredients
  • Impressive Wine List
  • Casual Neighborhood Spot

The Blue Plate opened in 1999 and was ennvisioned as a casual neighborhood spot where friends and neighbors could enjoy the best ingredients our seasons have to offer. The garden and dining rooms are each constructed from found objects, local materials and elbow grease. The comfortable interior frames the work of San Francisco artists and provides a casual venue for enjoying great food paired with great wine. At Blue Plate our kitchen always stays committed to using the highest quality local produce and meats in our New American and rustic Mediterranean dishes. We are only open for dinner and our menu changes daily as we work with local farmers to provide the freshest seasonal fare. We look forward to seeing you at Blue Plate for a glass of wine and some phenomenal food.

User reviews of Alembic

 "Great culinary and mixology art and top knotch service." by Yuka EIC (16 reviews)
December 02, 2008 - Likes it - I had the best meal of my life here. Combination of the service, what I got to see for mixology and culinary, and the hospitality. Congratulation on making the 2009 Michelin guide that is very well deserved.
The best meal of my life included:
- Scallops ceviche with tomato water, grilled corn, other heavenly things
- Japanese squid with ponzu dashi, myoga, yama gobo, smelt roe, absolutely best Japanese food I've had in US, great execution
- Little neck clams with heavenly sauce
-Potato chips and anchovies and grilled shishito (perfection!) these are on the menu and we had them last time too
-Albacore slider! Seared fish with bread! Amazing execution!
-Kobe beef tongue, soft and flavorful like ribs almost!
- Cheese plates with caramel olive sauce (!) as one of the accompaniment
For dessert we had their chocolate pudding, delicious! The whole course was accompanied by 4 beautiful cocktails (sorry can't remember the names but one with the celery juice and one with gold flakes and one with port and cognac and a tequila base one).  All of these were done by the master mixologist that likes to have fun Daniel.
Got to visit the kitchen and say hi to the chef and the pastry chef. Great! Thank you Kyla for your wonderful perfect timed out course service with Rachel again.
The Alembic, you have my absolute devotion from now on.

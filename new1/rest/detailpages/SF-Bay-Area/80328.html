 "Romantic, Cozy, & Delish" by Diahann (18 reviews)
May 15, 2009 - Really likes it - 3.5/4 stars! - The most romantic and cozy venue I've been to in SF. There are numerous areas to sit (just choose) where you can have quiet time just for the two of you...literally a beautiful refuge...and the music is great...there's a very talented live jazz band playing Thursday thru Saturday. "Sophistocated fare" - For appetizers, make sure to try the lobster ravioli...its the best arrangement of flavors...the halibut and the rack of lamb were cooked to perfection and excellent...a definite "must try".

 "Ana Mandara Rocks!" by Charles Lindauer (2 reviews)
September 10, 2008 - Really likes it - Great ambiance, unlike any I've experienced. World class bar, with a great jazz trio on weekends, and the best food I've ever eaten.

 "Great beer and food" by TheWeasel (59 reviews)
August 17, 2008 - Likes it - Walked over after grabbing 2 pints at Jupiter just a block and a half away. The place had a decent number of people around but wasn't overly busy in a Sunday night (it did clear out a bit more after the kitchen closed at 10pm).
Plenty of room at the bar and the bartender was really cool (he even gave me my last pint for free). 8 house beers on tap so I went for the sampler (they also had 2 cask brews which I didn't get a chance to try). All 8 are served up in 4oz pours so a sampler may be all you need. All were pretty good with a couple being great. Didn't checkout the food menu while I was there but plenty of people were enjoying the appetizers. Lots of pretty college girls around to chat with while you enjoy a drink. They have 1 gallon plastic cubes with a tap for off-sale which I hadn't seen before. A great place to enjoy a good beer and good company.

Message from Eccolo

Chez Panisse alum branches out on his own, bringing Italian rusticity to Fourth Street's main drag.

  • Extensive wine list
  • Brunch menu
  • Perfect for private parties

Eccolo serves Italian brunch, lunch and dinner and offers a wide selection of wines. An afternoon bar menu is offered until 5pm consisting of Tuscan fries with rosemary and sage, house-cured Italian meats and farmhouse cheeses, soups of the day and burgers.

 "Normally fabulous; when understaffed, eh - not so much" by ilovefood (3 reviews)
June 23, 2009 - Likes it - Took my dad here for Father's Day along with two other family members. Turns out, two wait staff did not show up for their shift and our server had 12 tables to cover. The food still rocked, but the entire meal - sit down 'til bill paid - took two hours. Very European I realize but the usual swift, attentive service (along with food that rocks) was absent. (had to ask for bread and water several times while waiting for the main dishes to arrive; no cheese on the cheeseburger, etc.)
I consider it a fluke and will be back, but wonder who are they hiring in this economy who feels they have the luxury of not showing up??

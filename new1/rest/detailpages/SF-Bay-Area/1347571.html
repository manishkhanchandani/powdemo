 "Awesome" by Steak Lover (1 review)
December 26, 2008 - First of all, as indicated by the address (Brentwood), Vic Stewart's is not in Antioch but in Brentwood. My guess is the confused Urbanspoon reviewers are from Antioch. The opening of Vic Stewart's, the Streets of Brentwood upscale shopping center, Shutters Brasserie, and the Contra Costa Wine Store in Brentwood probably has increased the tension with Antioch. My wife and I went to Vic Stewart's with another couple and we noticed that all of the folks from Brentwood, Discovery Bay, and Livermore loved the place and understood that, like Ruth's Chris or Vic Stewart's in Walnut Creek, everything is a la carte. All the food was incredible. We, and the other couple, noticed a small percentage of people from other towns (not Brentwood, Discover Bay, Livermore) that expected Vic Stewart's to be like the Outback chain. I assume those people will not return to Vic Stewart's which is probably for the best. The aged rib-eye ($46) is awesome and Vic Stewart's has an extensive wine list of many regional wines including excellent Brentwood wines (e.g., Hannah Nicole, etc.). The interior of the restaurant was spacious and beautiful. The service was impeccable. This fine, upscale steakhouse is amazing - we are going to return in about a week and gladly drop another $400. One last note - this Vic Stewart's has a beautiful bar.

 "A Little Piece of Heaven..." by Dennis (1 review)
November 17, 2008 - Really likes it - ...on earth. Gary Danko's restaurant is about as close to perfect as you can get. From calling to make reservations, to the greetings upon arrival, through the service throughout the meal to the departure...every detail receives attention. I can't think of anything to improve. On my first visit, I pretty much expected the food to be perfectly prepared and expertly presented...it was. My dining companion had a gluten allergy, which was handled sensitively and without fuss, everything was a matter of course.My big surprise came when I departed...they had sent for my car when the presented the bill...the valet had it waiting for me when I left. What a pleasant surprise! Don't omit the cheese course...it was exquisite.

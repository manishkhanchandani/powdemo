 "My goodness!" by Mimi (10 reviews)
March 08, 2009 - Likes it - I honestly must disagree with anyone who dislikes the Crab House. The only opposition I have is it's EXTREMELY pricey..for me, that is (: I come here when I have a long weekend nearing my birthday and drive up north to SF. I HAVE to stop here if I'm ever in the area for more than a night. The crab and the secret garlic butter? Oh god, I think I just died and went to heaven. Shirley temples are spiked with something delicious because I've NEVER had one taste like THAT before! (It's non-alcoholic, mind you!) Shrimp is delicious, service is amazing, and they also give you a fashionable bib! Gotta love Fisherman's Wharf and what fine dining it's got to offer ;D

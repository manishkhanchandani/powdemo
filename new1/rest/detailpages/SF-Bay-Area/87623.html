Message from Mi Lindo Peru

Mi Lindo Peru is Peruvian cooking at affordable prices.

  • Lots of hot & cold appetizers
  • Close to Bart, in the Mission
  • 2nd Location on Scott St.

We offer wonderful Peruvian and Spanish tapas and entrees for Lunch and Dinner. Some highlights include Ceviche, Anticuchos, Papa a la Huancaina, Lomo Saltado, Tallarin Saltado de Carne, Saltado de Pollo, Arroz con Pollo, Parihuela and Aguadito de Mariscos Wondering what all of these dishes are? They are deliciously flavored beef, chicken and seafood dishes that you must experience for yourself.

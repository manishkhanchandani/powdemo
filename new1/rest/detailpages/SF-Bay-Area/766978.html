Message from Wente Vineyards Restaurant

Eighteen championship holes, part of the PGA's national tour, set amongst the hills of Livermore valley next to the Wente Viney

  • First Rate Amenities
  • Superb Course Conditioning
  • Exceptional Guest Service

The Course at Wente Vineyards More than a decade ago, Wente Vineyards and golfing great Greg Norman joined forces to create The Course at Wente Vineyards, a destination championship golf course that is one of the most beautiful and challenging in Northern California wine country. Located 45 minutes east of San Francisco, The Course's 18 championship holes play through the vineyards, rolling hills, and natural grasslands of the scenic Livermore Valley at Wente Vineyards. Designed for players of all levels, The Course at Wente Vineyards is host of the Wine Country Championship, the 5th stop on the PGA?s Nationwide Tour. As our club guest your memorable golfing experience will include first rate amenities, superb course conditioning, and exceptional guest service. Come find the best of golf and the best of California!

 "I have had many great meals there" by Mike Ahmadi (15 reviews)
November 23, 2008 - Really likes it - I always take visitors to my hometown of Livermore to this landmark restauarant when I want to show off the beauty of my little part of the East Bay.
Wente Restaurant is nestled in "the hills" and isolated from just about everything that looks like a city.  The grounds are absolutely stunning and the food is quite delicious.
I have taken my wife there every Mother's Day since our first child was born 4 years ago, and will continue to do so.  The Mother's Day Brunch is a bit pricey, but the food is quite good.  This past (2008) Mother's Day I sent back my first meal ever.  It was a piece of Grilled Salmon which was served RAW.  I love sushi, but I like Grilled Salmon rare, not raw.  I sent it back twice, and they finally comped my meal and gave me a Filet Mignon instead, which was delicious.
Every restaurant has a bad day.  Overall this place is high on my list of great eateries.  I will be back.

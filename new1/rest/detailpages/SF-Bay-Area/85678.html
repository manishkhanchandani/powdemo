 "A Must!" by Janie (9 reviews)
April 15, 2009 - Likes it - In the historic and stately Claremont Hotel this restaurant puts out a champagne Sunday and Holiday brunch fit for royalty. A piano accompanimant with a striking East Bay view to the bay are ingredients to the ambiance here. The buffet is delicious, fresh and vast. The brunch is meant to be taken leisurely. Linger over the delectable desserts and coffee before taking a stroll in the gardens, club area and hotel's interior. For a real treat, stay the night in one of the quaint rooms or book a day at the spa! The hotel is one of my favorite destinations and the brunch a treat I enjoy each time I visit. Never a disappointment!

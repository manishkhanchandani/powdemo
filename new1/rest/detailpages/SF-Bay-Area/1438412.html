 "Father's Day Brunch" by karingh (1 review)
June 22, 2009 - We went to Zoey's for brunch on Father's Day thinking that we would be served some of the Mediterranean items on their menu. Instead, they had a selection of tired, steam-table entrees which can be found in cheap, cafeteria-style restaurants. Not only did the items on display look unappetizing, some were also cold. The only "hot" food that looked reasonable were the plain scrambled eggs and the home-style potatoes. The only choices for cold items were a sad-looking, wilted Ceaser Salad with too much dressing and a few choices of fruit. Needless to say, we were very sorry that we came here and are not likely to return. Perhaps all of the empty tables in the restaurant are an indication that other people already knew what we also know now. This place needs to get a new cook and figure out how to bring people in or we may notice, one of these days, that their sign has disappeared from the side of the building.

 "Love this place" by Nathaniel (1 review)
June 13, 2009 - Likes it - Wow - it seems some of these reviewers had a bad experience, but from the 5 or 6 times I've been there (and heading there again tonight for my birthday), I really enjoy Zoey's. I've only had one thing I didn't like (the tiramisu), but other items (like the Qabali and the clam chowder) are excellent. The Persian burger is really tasty too. Don't count it out because of one bad day. This place deserves to do well.

 "Sorry but it SUCKED!" by Julee (47 reviews)
April 16, 2009 - Doesn't like it - where do I start? it took 45 minutes to get our drinks. 50+ minutes to get our lunch when there were only 6 other people 4 which already had there lunch. the menu was all over the place with burgers (nothing normal about them featuring marinated beef fresh grated seasonings) Italian, kabobs, and seafood. the menu is a very pricey for lunch at $40 for two. I had the chicken Parmesan.... can we say frozen TV meal with some extra bottle sauce poured over the top? I really didn't see anything special about this place and will not be going back.

User reviews of Buckeye Roadhouse

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 "Solid food spotty service" by Bonnie L. (1 review)
April 20, 2009 - My guy is currently in love with this place so we've been here every sunday 3 weeks in a row. I believe the food is reasonably priced. Brunch dishes are about $12-16. BBQ items are about $18-$20 range. We had the filet mignon for about $35 comes with a delicious baked guyere cheese potato gartin and spinach. The skirt steak is about $22.50 and only ok.
Famous Oyster Bingo, spinach bed baked with cheese was good. I've had the famous smore pie and banana bread pudding and both were overly sweet.
Service is really spotty, they didn't bring us water when I asked twice. We were sitting next to another table and the waitress "Thanked" then and said "There you go" to us. The best service was from the valet guy who conversed with us and had a big smile.

 "Love those ribs" by Steve Blackwell (7 reviews)
March 31, 2009 - Likes it - This place is on my go-to list when we have a guest from out of town as there is something on the menu to cater to most tastes. Love the dungeness crab cakes and the baby back ribs.

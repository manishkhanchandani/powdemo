 "F*ing Amazing" by Jane Doe Eats - Seattle (127 reviews)
May 04, 2009 - Likes it - Had a two-dozen oystergasms at Hog Island Oyster Company in the Ferry building last month during the RSA conference, April '09. I relished every glorious gift from Alah/Buddha/Vishnu/Jesus as I sat at the Oyster bar gazing out over the sun drenched San Francisco Bay, watching the ferries. I had a dozen each of the Pacific Oysters and Kumamoto Oysters. I would have kept going had I not feared falling off the bar stool giggling with zinc and iodine induced silliness.
I was so in love with life after that I went insane in the ferry building, buying up Cowgirl Creamery cheeses, dried sausages and bottles and bottles of grassy green olive oil at the stalls in the Ferry Building. My arms almost fell off on the trip back to my hotel, then again when I trekked to the FedEx to ship goodies to loved ones across the country.

Message from Barceluna

Southern European Tapas in an Amazingly Vibrant Setting

  • Authentic Wood-Fired Pizzas
  • Extensive Wine List
  • Tantalizing New Cocktails

BarCeluna is now located in Alameda in the middle of the Historic Park Street District, around the corner from the new Alameda Civic Center parking structure and around the corner from the recently restored Historic Alameda Theatre. Our new location features an expanded space with a custom built bar & elegant outdoor patio. BarCeluna serves Southern European tapas and wood fired pizzas from our wonderful wood burning pizza oven. We feature an extensive wine list and fresh & creative cocktails. Happy Hour Daily from 5:30-6:30 & Nightly Drink Specials after 9pm.

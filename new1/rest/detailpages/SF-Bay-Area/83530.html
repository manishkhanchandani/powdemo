Message from El Patio

El Patio Original is Fremonts Favorite Mexican Restaurant

  • Festive Mexican Music
  • Private Dinning Room
  • Full Tequila Bar

Welcome to El Patio, Fremont's Mexican Restaurant. Leave your troubles behind and join the fiesta. El patio has it all, an incredible atmosphere, very good service, and great authentic Mexican food! El Patio has plenty of room so bring the whole family. The dinning room is adorned with an array of folk art and collectables, that combined with the festive Mexican music, immerses you in world in which all you have to do is relax and enjoy. Is there an event in your future? El Patio has a large private dinning room that can easily accommodate between 25 and 60 guests. So come on down and discover why El Patio is truly Fremont's Mexican Restaurant. Oh, did I mention they have a full Tequila Bar?

 mobile review by Frances M. Shockley (19 reviews)
April 16, 2009 - Doesn't like it - the service is fast, frantic almost. The food is classic diner fare. What I don't like is the fact that it is always crowded and they jam people in almost to the point of claustraphobia. Further, because of the long lines, I feel pressured to gulp my meal down so that they can put the next butt in my seat, so to speak. It'll do in a pinch, but not my favorite.

 mobile review by Blas Herrera (5 reviews)
April 05, 2009 - Likes it - Great corned beef hash! Pancakes were also really good. Easy in and out service was spot on and the coffee kept comming. Urban spoon has't let me down yet when in uncharted territories. Ate here after dropping my rabbit off to get spayed at the East Bay SPCA Clinic.

 "Waffles house serves great pancakes!" by Jamie (2 reviews)
March 29, 2009 - Likes it - Great PANCAKES, the waffles actually were not suggested by our waitress. FAST service and good, authentic breakfast fare. Lines going out the door most days...go early for a seat. Husband loved the hash browns, homemade and not overly salty like most places.

 "Greasy spoon" by Jason Toupes (15 reviews)
February 18, 2009 - Ole's is a one stop comfort stop. The menu consists of old fashion breakfast favorites along with the traditional meals. The fried chicken is highly recommended...I wouldn't say it's the best breakfast stop in alameda...but it wins with a old fashion feel. It could have an all you can challenge...like a 5 pound burger or 10 stacks of pancakes to round out the feel

Message from California Cafe

'Handsomely presented foods loaded...with flavor'-SJ Metro

  • Sunday Brunch
  • Seasonal Menu
  • Extensive Wine List

In the heart of Los Gatos Old Town shopping district, California Cafe has a beautiful oak-shaded outdoor patio and an expansive interior overlooking the lush Santa Cruz Mountains and the Los Gatos Creek Trail. Try our award-winning children's bento box, house-baked pretzel bread or any of chef's delicious seafood dishes. When you are in need of 'Liquid Therapy,' enjoy any of our exquisite California wines or lively signature cocktails. From large corporate affairs to intimate social gatherings California Cafe offers a variety of options including private and semi-private rooms, off-site catering, as well as the opportunity to buyout the entire restaurant.

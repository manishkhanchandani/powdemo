User reviews of Forbes Mill Steakhouse

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 mobile review by Tkirkman (4 reviews)
April 22, 2009 - I recently attended a salesmeeting in their banquet room. I must say it was an impressive and professional environment and the food was amazing. Kevin the manager made sure that every aspect of the event was to our liking. The menus were even customized! I'd also like to note the AV setup was new. Even though I was just attending the event I would recommend this restaurant to anyone looking to make a great impression on clients or even if just on a date.

 "Hit or miss" by Matt Alfano (2 reviews)
November 18, 2008 - Doesn't like it - I've had some good meals at Forbes Mill and some bad ones as well.  As far as the bad ones are concerned, my steak has been torched to the point of leather (and I order medium/medium-rare) and the sides bland and boring.  And while I'm not averse to paying up for quality, I have had sticker-shock here before (including visits where I could expense the whole meal....)

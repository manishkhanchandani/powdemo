Message from Beard Papa

A second SF destination for a cult cream-puff fix.

  • Cream puffs
  • Eclairs
  • Sweets & drinks

Many have called Beard Papa 'the world's best cream puffs'. Our Japanese style sweets are addictive thanks to our use of the freshest ingredients, our specialty pastry shells baked daily and our personal attention to customizing your order. The first U.S. based Beard Papa store opened in the exclusive Upper Westside of Manhattan in 2004 with much fanfare, attracting the attention of trend setters, visitors and locals looking for a 'sweet sensation'. Currently, operating franchises in New York, New Jersey, Boston, Hawaii, Los Angeles, and San Francisco with new stores planned across the US and Canada, Beard Papa is owned by Muginoho Co. Inc of Japan and has almost 300 outlets throughout Japan and Asia.

 "Thank you Beard Papa's Franchise Owner(s)!" by Estella Lai (17 reviews)
March 15, 2009 - Really likes it - As soon as I saw the face of the friendly beard papa, I forgot my caloric intake of the day. I first met this papa in a subway station in Hong Kong. I fell in love with beard papa immediately and I thought to myself, ok.. it's in the city and I could always come back. Then I moved to the US, of course, seeing papa again brought back many memories! I forgot how sweet and delicious each puff tasted, and the natural vanilla custard that challenges most mainstream cream puffs. I stuffed my face with one but as soon as the ecstasy was over I felt like I just swallowed a mountain... (because it was after dinner). Again, well worth it. They also had this promo going on where they offer one different flavor each week. I didn't get a chance to taste the green tea one but it's okay. They also had this little cream cheese cake stick thing, but naw, compared to the freshly made cream puffs, that thing didn't look a bit enticing. Oh, it made me very excited when the guy was filling the cream puffs; I think you could see the ovens in action as well.

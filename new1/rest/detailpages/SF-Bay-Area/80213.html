 "PURE SPANISH" by Jeff (23 reviews)
June 26, 2009 - Likes it - This place is cool. The Paella is very good...the family that runs the place is friendly, and the tapas' are all good. If you want a good Sangria, get a pitcher, then make sure you walk back to your hotel/motel. The Sangria is so strong, it could sink the Titanic. Great romantic restaurant that is well worth the time.

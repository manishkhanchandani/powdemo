 "Once had good steak and veggies" by Lisa Guinn (72 reviews)
April 10, 2009 - Doesn't like it - Okay -- I have to revise this review, as this place has gone downhill lately. The cooking is just not what it was, and kitchen management has fallen off. At a recent meal, one steak was terribly overdone, while another plate was stone cold. It took forever to get the overcooked steak replaced. They did comp the bad steak, but this is two disappointing experiences in a row. Once is a bad night, but two is a trend. Too bad.

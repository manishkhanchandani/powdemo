User reviews of Jardiniere

 "Excellent Food, Surprisingly Unpretentious" by Hoje (2 reviews)
June 20, 2009 - Really likes it - I had the charcuterie, some oysters, braised short ribs, and panna cotta, with a really enjoyable and reasonably priced Cotes du Rhone (d'Andezon). The charcuterie was varied enough to keep from turning into a Mess O' Meats. The short ribs were amazingly fatty and delicious! This is the kind of place that could so easily cross the line into arrogance and pretension, but everyone from the maitre d' to the sommelier was super friendly. You expect attentiveness, but the genuinely friendly vibe surprised me. True pros who take the enjoyment of their guests seriously.

 "Just what I'm looking for in a Cafe." by Mandy B. (1 review)
April 12, 2009 - Likes it - This is just what I'm looking for in a Cafe. Coffee, tea, beer & wine. All good, all cheap. AND good eats. I had the Mac & Cheese last night, and I have to say, it is better than the Claremont's Mac & Cheese (which is also very good!). My BF had the nachos, which were good, but he wanted more beans, less sour cream. The best thing about Grove Cafe is that they don't mind if you hang out. The management, the counter staff and especially the busboy are all nice and the customers are much cooler than the Marina-stereotype.

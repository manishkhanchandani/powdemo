User reviews of Presidio Social Club

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 "More than adequate food, tragically half-axed service" by j (47 reviews)
December 01, 2008 - Doesn't like it - The food was fine; a little overpriced, but more than adequate in the range of straight-ahead American choices the menu offers.
The service was a very different story, and makes this cute place more of an adventure than such an eatery needs to be.
Presidio Social Club has a very young greet-staff and wait-staff, apparently hired more for physical attractiveness than experience. Our party of 8 (a somewhat challenging factor) had an early reservation (5:30), because some people had other engagements to get to mid-evening. With the house less than 1/4 occupied, we got our order in at 6:03, main course arrived at 7:11 (house was more than half-full by then).
     Main course arrived too late for one person; tried to ask to have them not bring it out, but waitrons refused, since the cooking had already started. Not a graceful refusal, though not at all rude in delivery; just with the undertone that we were being difficult and uncooperative and w/zero recognition that they had had any part in the issue.
If the house can't handle either the logistics or the customer service wrapping at half-full, it needs a lot of work. *I* won't go back; I don't urge you to be that absolute...but choose your time there carefully if you care about service.

Message from Bin 38

BIN 38 named 'TOP 5 WINE BAR IN THE BAY AREA

  • Small Production Wines
  • Exotic Beers
  • Full Dinner Menu

Experience a whole New World of wine at BIN 38 Wine Bar & Restaurant. The wines of Argentina, Australia, California, Chile, New Zealand, Oregon, South Africa and Washington all take center stage. BIN 38 provides wine, beer and food lovers a prime new place to sip, swirl and snack in the Marina district. We also offer a rare find in the city ??A???A?A???A?A? two outdoor seating areas: a large, sunny outdoor back patio and a heated front sidewalk seating area. With 56 wines by the glass, finding a favorite should be a thoroughly enjoyable task. The hand-selected, small production wines are unique and hidden gems from across the New World. BIN 38 also offers a selection of 25 exotic beers featuring a wide array of premier international and local breweries for those who prefer bitter over brix.

 "cute little place" by muffintop1029 (104 reviews)
May 31, 2007 - Likes it - I love the atmosphere of this place--with its open kitchen, small counter inside and 2 picnic tables outside.  The duck breast sandwich with cole slaw that we had was flavorful and the duck well prepared.  I did not like the potato puffs (as my friend describes it--tater tots with mashed potato filling) as much as I thought I would given my love for potatoes prepared in both ways.  Would love to try some of the other sandwiches at this charming little place.

User reviews of Banana Leaf

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 "Best restaurant in the world!" by S H Guinn (3 reviews)
February 19, 2009 - Really likes it - I love all types of Asian cuisine. This place combines the best of Thai, Indian, Chinese, Malaysian. The Roti Prata, pineapple fried rice, mango chicken, wonderful curries, Tom Yaml Soup, great noodle dishes, and bananas granite are just a few of my favorites. Come early. Needless to say, they do get busy.

Recommend this review

 "Really Really Like it!" by Lisa Guinn (72 reviews)
February 17, 2009 - Really likes it - If you are coming for lunch, show up early or be prepared to wait!  Dinner can be crowded too, although they move things along pretty well.  The place is lively and bustling most of the time.  It's not cheap, but well worth the price.  Must-haves: Roti Prata, Mango Chicken, Pineapple Fried Rice, Yu Sang Delight (vegetarian).  We eat here often and have never been disappointed.

Recommend this review

 "AMAZING" by Abhi (1 review)
December 06, 2008 - Likes it - This place is amazing. They have excellent dishes, great services and an array of foods to choose from for vegetarians and non-veg's. The price is a tad high but its worth it. Perfect location for celebrating anniversaries, birthdays or just a great Malaysian restaurant to go to.

Recommend this review

 "Delicious!" by TigerXtreme (1 review)
September 13, 2008 - Really likes it - This is the first restaurant we went to after actually spinning the "Urban Spoon" iPhone app. And wow! The food was great; the service was excellent; the overall experience was really terrific. The two pleasant surprise foods were the Malay Rice (with coconut milk) and the Indian bread with some killer sauce. We will definitely be returning.

Message from Capurro's Restaurant & Bar

Classy seafood dining on the historic Port Walk at Fisherman's Wharf.

  • Live Music
  • Daily Specials
  • Happy Hour

Since 1946, Capurro's Restaurant has been family owned and operated. Capurro's offers a traditional Fisherman's Wharf menu reminiscent of our Italian heritage. Serving breakfast, lunch and dinner we feature only the freshest locally caught seafood. Featuring our own three-piece band--dedicated to the music of the Golden Era--five nights a week, Capurro's offers a dining experience unlike any other.

 "MY BEST EVER CRAB MEAL" by Jason Brewer (5 reviews)
December 29, 2008 - Really likes it - Today I went to Capurro's as its next to my Hotel the Argonaugt on Fisherman's Wharf. We went for a late lunch and cocktails. My girl friend ordered a Blueberry Martini it was very good very strong loaded with booze!  I had a local Beer also v good.
For starter we ordered a mixed starter consisting of fried Calamari, 2 King Prawns  both good and one Crab Cake with a sweet tomato based sauce (which was fabulous) that we shared between us. The Crab cake was not dry and heavy like i expected it to be, i thought it looked way too moist and wet which made me raise an eyebrow to say the least, but the moment i tasted the sweet crab cake i realized i was in safe hands at Cupurro's, because the crab cake alone was the work of genius!!! It was packed full of crab flavour.  
For main course my girl friend had Clam Chowder served in a Sour Dough Bread Bowl It was wonderful, i have never had a local  SF Bay clam chowder, so to be fair to any one reading this i guess i am not an authority on the best chowder thats out there but on a personal view, if i was served that soup every time i ordered it i would be a very very happy man it was thick creamy flavoursome  and slightly salty to let the flavour of the sea develop on your taste buds.  It was so good I had to keep stealing spoons of her dish its was memorable.
I move on to the Dungeness crab they roast it in a wood fired oven (i think its a pizza oven) and it sure makes for good crab! I would like to explain that crab is my personal favorite seafood  i always order it if it think it will be good. Any way the crab was served with a classic butter, garlic and white wine reduction that was so good i was forced to stop cracking the crab claws open and then eat the snowy white meat immediately instead i patiently  cracked all the claws filled the bottom of my bowl with the meat and let it marinade in the sauce. I didnt leave a single peace of meat behind in the shell, by the time i was done i had a fair mound of crab meat in my bowl and could tuck in the the white goodness! Eating the crab in that sauce was one of the culinary highlights of my life,  a real taste sensation i will never forget it ! $19.95 for 1lb crab (it seemed bigger to be honest but i am not sure) i just know i was in heaven!  Service was also good.

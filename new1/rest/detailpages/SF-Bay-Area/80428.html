 "I have eaten there twice" by Mike Ahmadi (15 reviews)
November 23, 2008 - Really likes it - The first meal I had there was in 2000, and it was one of the best meals of my life (beat The French Laundry, hands down).  The second time I ate there was with my Wife in 2007 for our second anniversary, and it was good, but not outstanding.
I blew my server away by not only knowing that the chocolate they had used to write "Happy Anniversary" on my dessert plate was Valrhona, but I even knew that it was Valrhona Pur Caribe.

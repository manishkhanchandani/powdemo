Message from Hana Zen

Lively Japanese restaurant plies Union Square tourists and late-night diners with sushi, yakitori and sake cocktails.

  • Dine-in/takeout Japanese food
  • Extensive Sake Menu
  • Good for Groups

At Hana Zen, we're about great service, great sake, and great sushi! As one of Union Square's favorite Japanese restaurants, we proudly serve the freshest fish with the finest ingredients. To accompany your meal, try an exotic sake cocktails or indulge in explore our huge selection of authentic saki choices. Looking for something more adventurous to tantalize your tastebuds? Order off our full yakitori menu!

User reviews of Hana Zen

 "Get the Dragon Roll" by Matthew (32 reviews)
December 22, 2008 - Likes it - Before I tried Sushi Bistro, I went to Hana Zen. They were recommended to me by my hotel's concierge. After arriving, I went to use the restroom to wash my hands. The restroom had no handsoap. After informing the hostess, she brought me a hot towel to wipe my hands with, saying that she didn't know where the soap was. This was a little disturbing. I'm guessing those in the kitchen and behind the sushi bar had access to handsoap.
That very small complaint aside, the sushi rolls were pretty good. I enjoyed several of their special rolls, with the Dragon Roll being my favorite.

Message from Puccini & Pinetti

A Union Square trattoria known for its high-energy atmosphere and finger food.

  • Lunch, dinner, happy hour
  • Large parties welcome
  • Kid-friendly and fun

We serve a fabulous Happy Hour, with a variety of domestic and imported tap beers, and house wine for only $3.00. We also offer tasty appetizers like Pasta Nachos, Bruschetta, and our Pizza of the Day for only $3.00. Check out Puccini & Pinetti's Lunch Club. Be a regular and you'll always have a seat and we'll occasionally treat you to lunch! See our website for more information or ask your server. Let us know that you found us on Citysearch.

User reviews of Puccini & Pinetti

 "Amazing Italian Food and Atmosphere" by Mary Stanger (2 reviews)
June 15, 2009 - Likes it - First, the tapenada they delivered with the bread was so good I could have just eaten it all evening!! The Bruschetta with the goat cheese was the best I've tasted and my entree of Halibut was done to perfection. I shared my Tiramisu, since everything was so good, I didn't want to explode!! The atmosphere was great. It was really busy the evening we were there, but we didn't feel rushed or neglected. If I lived in San Francisco, I would make this one of my regulars.

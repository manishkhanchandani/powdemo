 "Good Food, Dismissive Attitude" by Hoje (2 reviews)
June 19, 2009 - Doesn't like it - I had the halibut tartare, goat shoulder, & pluot tart, which were all fine. Being the meat of the Third World, I was surprised to find the goat better than any I'd had abroad.
HOWEVER, both our maitre d' and our server were at best brusque, at worst rude. The word "please" is apparently not part of their vocabulary, as in "please have a seat." Instead we were told to "just sit there." Menus were not handed to us; they were thrown onto the table. When our table was ready, the maitre d' simply grabbed the menus out of our hands and walked off without saying anything. We could see that other guests were being treated in the same manner, which might have been less odd if it had been extremely busy or if it were a $5 taqueria. But for a couple hundred bucks, I was expecting better. I will not be going back.

March 03, 2009 - Really likes it - 4/4 Stars! - I visited Chez Panisse for the first time on a Thursday evening. Although...the price goes up as the week progressess, it is well worth it. My favorite restaurant in the East Bay, thus far. Only downside is finding available parking on the street. Pricey - $60+ and up as the week progresses

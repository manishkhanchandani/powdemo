 "Haddock & Chips!! MMMMMM!!" by Glenda (1 review)
February 18, 2009 - Likes it - Best in the Bay Area by far!!  Home away from home, wonderful food, service and staff.  They added the Haddock to the menu a few months ago and I haven't had anything else since it's that GOOD.  Great place to meet up with friends and family for dinner.  We watch all the local sporting events here too.
I love that the owners arrange outings for the locals, we been horse racing, Sharks games and lots of concerts where dinner and transport have been included with the tickets price!! No worries, what else could you ask for??

Recommend this review

 "Great atmosphere, good food..." by Ann E. Onn (1 review)
February 16, 2009 - Likes it - This pub has a great atmosphere, and good, good food (not to mention good drinks).  They have their own smoker and so make some of the best BBQ sandwiches -- the pulled pork is great.  They also deviate a bit from traditional pub grub -- as well as having the usual favorites, the other night they had a really tasty fondue!  The Kobe burgers are delicious too.  Service is always good.  Bar staff are a pleasure!

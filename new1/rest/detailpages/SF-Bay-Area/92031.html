Message from Tangerine

Romantic dining and relaxing weekend, holiday brunch in the Castro.

  • Pacific Rim cuisine
  • Extensive wine list
  • Patio seating

Tangerine, Pacific Rim Cuisine is defined as a blend of flavors and styles of food from countries that border the Pacific Ocean. Chef Sean Pattansuvoranun selects the freshest meats, seafood, vegetables and spices used in those countries to create his Pacific Rim cuisine.

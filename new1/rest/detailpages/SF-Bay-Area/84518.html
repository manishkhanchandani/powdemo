Message from Greens

The sacred cow of upscale vegetarian restaurants and a San Francisco tradition.

  • Extensive wine list
  • Selection of pastries
  • Sandwiches, soups & salads

Green's Restaurant overlooks the Marina at the Golden Gate Bridge and features creative vegetarian cuisine using the finest seasonal organic produce and high quality ingredients. The restaurant maintains a close relationship with its purveyors. The Zen Center's Green Gulch Farm and Bolinas' Star Route Farms are two frequently used sources. Greens emphasizes serving outstanding dishes created with a balance of colors, flavor and contrast of textures. The presentation is simple yet stylish. The cuisines of the Mediterranean, Mexico and the American Southwest influence the full flavored foods. Greens' wine list offers wine by the bottle, half bottle, or glass. Greens Restaurant has a list that is notable among wine professionals as well as diners for its depth, breadth and precision.  "Tasty, elegant, green" by Amber Gregory (15 reviews)
March 08, 2009 - Likes it - Greens is elegant, gourmet, haute vegetarian cuisine at its (nearly) finest. My husband and I went to Greens for his birthday one year, and our dining experience was wonderful. It was a mellow dining environment and we did not feel like we were sharing all of our conversations with people at other tables.
The food was wonderful. It is so nice to find a nice restaurant that caters to vegetarians, yet gives you that same dining experience that you get in more meat-oriented fine dining establishments. My only complaint is that I felt most dishes were a little heavy on the cheese and other dairy, and this is something that surprised me. I guess I imagined that a vegetarian restaurant would not douse things in cheese--not that there's anything wrong with that (I adore cheese!)--but it just didn't seem as "healthy" as I had been expecting.

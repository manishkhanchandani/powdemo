Message from Espetus

Authentic Brazillian Steakhouse--all the meats you can eat.

  • Authentic Brazilian cuisine
  • Rodizio style restaurant
  • Meat and salads

The flavors of Brazil come alive at EspetuS with San Francisco's very first Rodizio Style restaurant. The concept is simple: serve the meat straight from the fire on which it was cooked so that the flavor, temperature and texture are perfect. In the Brazilian Language, EspetuS means skewer. In a traditional churrascaria (shoo-ras-ka-ria), meat is cooked on skewers over an open fire and brought straight to the table where it is carved directly onto your plate. For one set price, you will experience one or all of our succulent cuts of meat, continuously served until you are finished eating. EspetuS also has a full salad bar overflowing with the exotic salads and side dishes of the Brazilian cuisine.

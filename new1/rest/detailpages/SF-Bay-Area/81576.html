User reviews of Cafe Borrone

Share your opinion with the world. Add your review below.

 "Delicious!" by Tamsin Harriman (1 review)
January 23, 2009 - Really likes it - Probably my favorite cafe in the Bay Area. Great coffee, great food (try the steak churrasco sandwich, or any breakfast item), and great atmosphere. Their food is a little pricey, but largely worth it. They pull an excellent espresso shot (sadly, truly delicious espresso is hard to find). Their whipped cream is heavenly! I sometimes get the hot chocolate just so I can have whipped cream. ;)

Not just another Italian restaurant Palio is one of the great restaurants of San Francisco. Caroline Bates, Gourmet Named after the famous Medieval bareback horse race (Il Palio) in the town of Asti Italy, Palio D'Asti is a top-rated Italian Restaurant located in San Francisco's Financial District and within walking distance of all major hotels. The award- winning 7,000 sq. foot restaurant interior permits the viewing of the chefs as they prepare Italian dishes with particular emphasis on the regions of Piemonte and Toscana.

 "yum-ooo!!!" by ms. heather (1 review)
May 05, 2009 - my kids and i stopped in to see what the new place was like since the demise of louisiana's fried chicken. the place was bright and cheery, as well as the pleasant staff. while waiting for our order to be completed the store owner/chef greeted me and was very hospitable (nice smile too). when our food was ready, i took it home, but the ride back to antioch was agony because what i had in my bag smelled like heaven!!! could i wait till i got home.??? i waited and it was worth the wait. the fried chicken was juicy and tasty and seasoned perfectly. the potato salad was like the kind my mom used to make, the macaroni and cheese very velvety and creamy, the greens were wonderful too! the cornbread was like cake, perfect for cornbread and milk....i would reccomend this restaurant to anyone.....very clean as well....keep up the good work , this community needs a little soul put back in it and sophias is where to find it!!!

 "Sophia's Southern Cafe of Pittsburg, CA" by Msaejackson (1 review)
March 03, 2009 - Doesn't like it - Disappointment, doesn't even come close to what I can say in regards to Sophia's Southern Café. For Black History my employer catered lunch for all of its employees (over 300 people) from Sophia. I am very upset with what was served to the employees. To start the chicken was not cooked all the way. I bit into a wing only to find that it had not been fully cooked, red to the bone. The yams although seasoned wonderfully every 2/3 bits included burned yams (black in color). It was as if a burned pan was mixed in, with the pans that came out ok so the full amount was delivered. The green had no and I am serious when I say No Seasoning what so ever. The greens tasted as if they were, I hope washed and then just added to water and cooked till tender. Now for the Mac & Cheese, I had no idea anyone could mess up Mac and Cheese till today. They would have been better off bringing us boxed Mac & Cheese. To top all of this off the food was served cold because the staff forgot to bring the burners to keep the food warm.
The owners of Sophia's need to be ashamed of them self for serving this kind of slop. Not only myself but several other co-workers became ill after eating small amounts of what was served. I am sickened by anyone who would rush preparation of food just to turn a quick buck.
Well Sophia's the joke was on us, and you laughed all the way to the bank.

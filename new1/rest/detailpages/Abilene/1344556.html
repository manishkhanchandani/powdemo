 "Disappointment" by bigrod (15 reviews)
April 10, 2009 - Doesn't like it - We had heard how great Beehive was and finally decided to pay it a visit. What a mistake! I don't know if it was just a bad night or what but at those prices we will NOT return. I must say that our server was fantastic, there was absolutely nothing wrong with the service. The food was a different story, from start to end, it was disappointing...sans the salad and soup. We ordered a combination tempura type veggie tray (mushrooms, zucchini, cauliflower, and onion rings). It should have NEVER left the kitchen. It was burned, so soaking with oil that it gleaned. We didn't finish it. Next came a $30 hunk of meat and bone that was supposed to be Prime Rib. It was such an unappetizing looking hunk of grayish looking mystery meat. It looked like it was among a bunch of other meat that was parboiled and left to sit in the water. My wife cut into it, took a bite, and it was cold. She is not one to send food back but had to. She waited a few minutes and a larger piece of disgusting looking flesh was delivered, there was not much difference between it and the first.
I ordered the Oscar beef (it had Oscar in its name). It consisted of 3 beef medallions wrapped in bacon, covered with crab and some sort of creamy, yellowish sauce. I ordered it medium (should have been warm and pink), but could see blood oozing from the top of the meat. I cut into it and it was red, not pink. I will admit that it was flavorful and very, very tender. I chose to not send mine back and dug into it a bit more. It would have been a decent meal had it not been covered in crab and the yellow sauce. The crab was mushy and chalky if you can believe it. I've eaten seafood all over the country and overseas and have NEVER had crab that had this texture or consistency. It was also very fishy. The sauce was bitter and sour, not good at all, and had a piece of hair or something in it.
Don't plan on getting free soda refills either...ridiculous if you ask me, especially when you are paying prices like these. We were given a piece of free cheesecake and one free soda refill as an honest attempt by our server to offer some sort of compensation. Really we should have called a manager over and refused the meals.
Needless to say, we won't visit again. I am actually rather angry for paying almost $80 (tip included) for such a disappointing meal.

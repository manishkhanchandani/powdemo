 "AWESOME!" by bigrod (15 reviews)
January 28, 2009 - Really likes it - My family and I were elated to see a sushi/hibachi joint opening in Abilene. After spending 5 years in Okinawa, Japan and eating sushi regularly we were hoping to get something close to the "real deal" and Bonzai delivers!! We are regulars there and find that the quality has not faltered. The sushi is phenomenal, some of it is a bit Americanized but it is amazingly fresh and tastes great. The hibachi (known as tepanyaki in Japan) is good as well. There is something for everyone. This place is busy most of the time which makes me hope they'll open a sushi only Bonzai. They have only a few tables for those who don't want to sit around the hibachi tables so a sushi only restaurant would be awesome.
